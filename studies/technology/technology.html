TECHNOLOGY TEAM

The Technology team responsible for managing creek data and publicizing the Arcade Creek Project through informational videos and the website.

Tech is organized into three subdivisions, Data, Website, and Video.

Data keeps track of all the hours of Arcade Creek Project members using Bento, a database program. In addition to putting together the rosters and contact lists at the beginning of the year, data also manages mandatory attendance at events such as lectures and restoration outings. The primary responsibility of Website is the maintenance of the Creek website. It provides an overview of the project to the public and updates the students on upcoming deadlines and events. Video is in charge of producing informative visual media, collaborating with other study groups to create study group videos, direction videos, and an introductory video for the project.

Additionally, Tech works closely with Outreach to publicize the project through providing media such as informational brochures. Members also take pictures to document the progress made by the entirety of the Arcade Creek Project.
