Grants and Awards

The Arcade Creek Project has received many acknowledgements over the years including:

  • Sacramento Urban Creek Council Creek Stewardship 1999, 2005, and 2009 Awards
  • 2004 Government's Enviornmental and Economic Leadership Award
  • Grant's Advisory Board for Youth (GABY) Award for 2008-2010 under the umbrella of the Sacramento Region Community Foundation and the Sierra Health Foundation

There have also been many generous groups that have given the project grants to fulfill our goal:

  • The Sacramento River Cats Educational Grant 2008
  • Financial support from Wells Fargo
